Living Wage pay for Your Cleaners

The cost of living in London is high, and yet so many of the jobs that the capital relies upon are poorly paid.

With Evo you can rest assured that, as well as fair pay, your cleaners will be able to access all of the benefits that everyone should be able to take for granted, like paid annual leave, compassionate leave, a proper employment contract and proper training. Unfortunately in the cleaning industry this is not always the case.

When you receive your quotation from Evo Cleaning Services, we will always include an option for your cleaner to be paid the London Living Wage, with a clear explanation of what your cleaner will be paid with and without choosing this option.
