Post-Builders Cleaning

Builders are great at construction... not so good at cleaning. Evo are a CIS registered cleaning provider with a health & safety conscious approach to work and over a decade's experience of working with construction teams to get sites looking spotless after the last snags and loose ends have been finsihed.

Our plan for your cleaning...

Evo will draw up a plan to cater for your specific requirements and will work at a time that is most convenient for you and your staff. Your Account Manager will take personal responsibility for the cleaning work at your site, and will make regular site visits in order to audit the work of our cleaners. Your Account Manager will also be contactable by you at any time, and will be keen to meet with you on a regular basis to discuss our work.

Our service always includes:

  • CoSHH trained, uniformed and experienced cleaning staff
  • Our Personal Management System
  • Provision of all equipment/consumables included in the price
  • A responsible approach to Health & Safety
  • Environmentally aware working practices
